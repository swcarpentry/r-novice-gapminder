Lesson plans

Guidelines

Each lesson must be stored in its own separate repository with a gh-pages branch. The lesson template repository can be found here: https://github.com/swcarpentry/lesson-template

Lesson

See: https://github.com/swcarpentry/lesson-template/blob/gh-pages/LAYOUT.md

A lesson has: - prerequisites - a series of topics (10-15 minute lessons followed by exercises) - Motivation slides - A reference guide - A page of general discussions - An instructor guide

Schedule

4 x 90 minute blocks gives time for 24 x 15 minute topics or 6 lessons with 4 topics each. or 8 lessons with 3 topics each.
